Shipping & Preorders

Every piece is made to order via Pickup Mtaani. Because production only begins once your deposit is confirmed, orders are placed in two payments.
01

Pay 70% deposit

Sent directly to our M-Pesa Paybill to confirm and begin your order.

02

We make your piece

You'll be notified once it's finished and ready.

03

Pay 30% balance

Covers delivery via Pickup Mtaani. Paid once your item is ready.

Please note: due to the made-to-order nature of each piece, all payments are non-refundable once production has begun.

About VINED

VINED is a Nairobi-based fashion house dedicated to slow, intentional craftsmanship — stay vain, stay you. Every piece is made to order, ensuring that each garment is crafted with care and attention to detail.

We believe in timeless design, quality materials, and the beauty of African creativity. Our collections are designed for the modern woman who values authenticity and elegance.
